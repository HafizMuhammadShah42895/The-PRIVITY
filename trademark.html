Registration Process

A step-by-step guide to securing your trademark

1. Search & Analysis

We conduct a thorough search to ensure your mark is available and doesn't infringe on existing rights.

2. Filing Application

We prepare and file your application with the appropriate trademark office, ensuring all details are correct.

3. Examination

We handle all correspondence with examiners and respond to any objections or office actions.

4. Registration

Once approved, your trademark is registered, granting you exclusive rights to use the mark.
